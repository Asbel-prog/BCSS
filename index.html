About Us

Trusted on the Road Since Day One

Bungoma Classic Shuttle was founded with a single mission: to give Western Kenya travellers a reliable, comfortable, and affordable way to move across the country. From our base in Bungoma, we operate modern, air-conditioned coaches staffed by professional, courteous crew.

We understand that every journey matters — whether it's a business trip to Nairobi, a family visit to Kisumu, or a student returning for the semester.

  • Modern, air-conditioned fleet
  • Professionally trained and vetted drivers
  • Punctual departures, every time
  • Affordable fares with no hidden costs
  • Luggage handling & parcel delivery

Scheduled Shuttle Routes

Private Charter Hire

Planning a group trip, company outing, or special event? Hire one of our coaches exclusively for your group — any route, any date.

Parcel & Cargo Delivery
